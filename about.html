About our foundation

We Rescue Birds & Animals In Need Around The City

Welcome to Shree Ganesh Foundation - Animal and Birds Rescue Center, where compassion meets action. Nestled amidst serene surroundings, our sanctuary is a haven for creatures great and small, offering refuge, care, and a second chance at life.

Embracing our urban ecosystem, we tirelessly rescue and rehabilitate birds and animals in need, safeguarding their well-being and harmony within our cityscape.

Hardik Patel, Founder of Shree Ganesh Foundation.
